## Overview

An internal developer platform (IDP) that gives engineers self-service access to infrastructure provisioning, service deployment, and observability — without filing ops tickets.

## Problem

Our engineering org was spending thousands of engineering hours per quarter waiting for infrastructure changes. The bottleneck was a small platform team handling every request manually.

## Solution

Designed and built a platform UI backed by a declarative infrastructure API. Engineers define their service requirements in a simple form or YAML config, and the platform handles provisioning via Terraform and deployment via GitHub Actions.

Key capabilities:
- One-click service provisioning (RDS, SQS, S3, ECS)
- Environment management (dev, staging, prod)
- Secret management via AWS Secrets Manager
- Deployment history and rollback

## Impact

- Reduced time-to-provision from 5 days → 15 minutes
- Eliminated 90% of platform team toil
- Onboarded 60+ engineering teams within 3 months
